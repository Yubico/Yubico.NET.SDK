## Get the YubiKey version

### Inner command APDU info

CLA | INS | P1 | P2 | Lc | Data | Le
:---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---:
00 | 06 | 00 | 00 | 8 | *data* | (absent)

The data is 8 random bytes.

### Response APDU info

Total Length: 5\
Data Length: 3

         Data          | SW1 | SW2

:---------------------:| :---: | :---:
*major, minor, patch* | 90 | 00
